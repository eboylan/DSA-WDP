What features does it have?

Come Fly With Me features a range of hardware and software solutions to provide truly striking drone photography and video.

Utilising state of the art stealth and camera technology to deliver a bespoke, candid and professional photography service, while ensuring the safety and comfort of guests.

Drone network offers live cloud backup to user as well as entire event site.

  • Navigation sensors to move safely at all (reasonable) altitudes indoor and outdoor.
  • Light sensors to facilitate optimum photographic positioning.
  • Capability to run pre-set ‘follow-me’ routines targeted on an individual or individuals predefined through facial recognition.
  • Pre-set routines include type of photography (panoramic/group, close-up), drone movement (e.g. constant hover for light optimisation or 360-degree circling) and roaming to capture all participants and the location.
  • Coupled with separate mini-controllers to engage/disengage, return to base and change target or pre-set routine.
  • On board Wi-Fi network allows guests to add photographs taken by smartphone or camera.
  • Advanced Collision Detection and Avoidance software to ensure safety of guests and property.
  • Brushless and soundproofed motors to reduce noise disruption.
  • High resolution 360-degree cameras offering multiple camera angles at same time, greatly adding to editing capabilities and enabling production of immersive, virtual reality wedding videos.
